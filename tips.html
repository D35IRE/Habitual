Sustainability Guide

🏠 At Home

  • Switch to LED bulbs - use 75% less energy
  • Unplug devices when not in use
  • Take shorter showers (5 minutes max)
  • Use cold water for washing clothes
  • Install a programmable thermostat
  • Seal air leaks around windows and doors

🚲 Transportation

  • Walk or bike for trips under 2km
  • Use public transportation
  • Carpool or use ride-sharing
  • Keep car tires properly inflated
  • Combine multiple errands in one trip
  • Work from home when possible

🍽️ Food & Diet

  • Eat more plant-based meals
  • Buy local and seasonal produce
  • Reduce food waste
  • Compost organic waste
  • Grow your own herbs and vegetables
  • Choose organic when possible

♻️ Waste Reduction

  • Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers
  • Recycle paper, plastic, glass, and metal
  • Buy products with minimal packaging
  • Repair items instead of throwing away
  • Donate or sell items you no longer need
  • Choose quality over quantity

💧 Water Conservation

  • Fix leaks promptly
  • Install low-flow showerheads
  • Collect rainwater for plants
  • Turn off tap while brushing teeth
  • Use dishwasher only when full
  • Water plants during cooler hours

🛒 Conscious Shopping

  • Buy only what you need
  • Choose sustainable brands
  • Look for eco-friendly certifications
  • Buy second-hand when possible
  • Support local businesses
  • Read labels and ingredients
